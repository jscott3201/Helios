# Safe research practices

- Do not expose secrets, credentials, tokens, private keys, or customer data in outputs.
- Prefer read-only operations for research and review.
- Ask before running destructive commands, migrations, deployment commands, or commands that modify external systems.
- For external research, prefer primary sources and record version/date.
- For benchmarks, capture environment and avoid comparing incompatible machines or workloads as direct A/B results.
- For compliance, report evidence and gaps; do not certify compliance unless your organization has a formal certification process.
